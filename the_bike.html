The Bike

Year: 1975

Make: Honda

Model: CB200T

Displacement: 200cc

We found these Honda CB200T motorcyles on Craigs List around 2010. The owner had it stored outside in the rain for most of it's life. He said had bought 2 one for him and one for his wife. But after his wife crashed they abandoned them and never rode them again. We paid $300 for both bikes non running and had big plans for them.
